Theoretical Framework

One of the challenges of any scholarly writing at the graduate level or beyond is to incorporate a discussion of relevant critical theories that will inform your work. The goal of this assignment is to develop the theoretical framework that will inform your final project.

  • Here are parameters:
    • Research the theories you find most relevant for your essay.
    • Write the introduction to the essay you're writing about.
    • Write a 2-4 page theoretical framework for your essay.
    • Reference foundational and recent discussions of them.
